Global Warming Analysis

Introduction

As you can see, the color of each geographic region changes obviously when you slide the time slider from 1900 to 2010. According to the research, the average global temperature has increased almost 1 degree centigrade from 1900.

In this analysis, we will analyze the cause of global Warming from three aspects: Population, Greenhouse Gan, & Forest Area.

In the end, we will discuss the impact of global warming.

Population

From the left visualisation, it can be seen that 9 countries with population of more than 100 million were added.

From the right visualisation, we can see that the total population increased around 3.5 billion people. The average temperature increased with the population.

More population means more carbon dioxide, more land occupation, more tree cutting, more ocean fishing, more environmental pollution.

Human has wreaked havoc on the ecological environment which is one of the main reasons of global climate change.
